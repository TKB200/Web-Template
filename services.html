Feed Nutritional Analysis

Mycotoxin Analysis

Rapid screening tests to determine aflatoxins, DON (Deoxynivalenol), Fumonisins, Ochratoxin, and Zearalenone in animal feed raw materials. Our immunoassay-based screening platforms provide fast, reliable detection of these harmful fungal metabolites critical for animal health and food safety compliance.
