Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a sample for analysis?

Contact our team via the form above or email us directly. We'll provide you with sample submission guidelines, required documentation, and logistics details.

How long does analysis take?

Turnaround time depends on the complexity of the analysis. NIR-based tests can be completed same-day; most microbiological and chemical analyses are completed within 3–7 business days.

What certifications do you hold?

Medivesta Laboratories is certified with GLP (Good Laboratory Practice) and ISO 17025, ensuring the highest standards of analytical accuracy and traceability in all our testing services.

Do you serve international clients?

Yes. As a member of the Dyvesta Group with international partnerships, we regularly work with clients across South Asia and beyond. Contact us to discuss international sample logistics.
